About Us

Who We Are

S&K Steel Furniture is a proudly Malawian furniture manufacturer established in 1966 in Blantyre. The company was born out of a simple yet ambitious dream — to craft durable, comfortable, and stylish furniture locally, at a time when most products were being imported. Starting with only four hardworking individuals in a modest 20x20 ft workshop, the founders laid a strong foundation built on dedication, skill, and the desire to provide reliable furniture solutions for the Malawian people.

In the early years, the company relied mainly on raw materials imported from Zimbabwe and specialized in manufacturing steel furniture such as desks, chairs, and cabinets. As the business grew in reputation and expertise, the company expanded into wood furniture production in the early 1970s, combining steel and timber to meet the growing market demand.

By the 1980s, S&K Steel Furniture had become one of Malawi’s most respected furniture brands, employing more than 100 skilled workers and supplying products to schools, government institutions, banks, and corporate offices across the country. However, as the years went by, the company faced new challenges — particularly the influx of cheap imported furniture and the global economic slowdown, which affected local production and sales.

Despite these difficulties, S&K Steel Furniture remained resilient. Through innovation, improved craftsmanship, and a commitment to maintaining local production, the company has continued to serve both public and private sectors with excellence. Today, the company operates with a dedicated team of 100 experienced employees, providing customized furniture solutions that meet international standards while reflecting true Malawian workmanship.
